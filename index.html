about

Influenced by the surf and skate culture from my home, the sunny west coast of Australia and inspired by the concept of japanese shibori. The results are a trippy and funky aesthetic of psychedelic colours and organic and geometric patterns. Currently residing in Berlin, the electronic music and open airs have also influenced the aesthetic.
